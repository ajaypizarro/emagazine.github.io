Daniil Sergeyevich Medvedev

is a Russian professional tennis player. He is currently ranked as world No. 2 by the Association of Tennis Professionals. He was ranked as world No. 1 for three weeks in 2022. He has won 13 ATP Tour singles titles, including the 2021 US Open and 2020 ATP Finals.

Born: February 11, 1996 / Moscow, Russia

Height: 1.98 m (6 ft 6 in)

Coach: Gilles Cervara

Prize money: US$23,585,311

Turned pro: 2014

Residence: Monte Carlo, Monaco

Siblings: Only child of (Sergey Medvedev, Olga Medvedeva)

Sponsors

Medvedev is endorsed by Lacoste for apparel and shoes, Tecnifibre for racquets, and Bovet for watches.


He also [for the Russian-speaking world mostly] has been employed as an ambassador by BMW, Tinkoff Bank, and HyperX for gaming accessories.
