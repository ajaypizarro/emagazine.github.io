Carlos "Carlitos" Alcaraz

is a Spanish professional tennis player. He has been ranked as high as world No. 6 in singles by the Association of Tennis Professionals.
Born: May 5, 2003 (age 19 years), El Palmar, Murcia, Spain
Height: 1.85 m
Coach: Juan Carlos Ferrero
Prize money: US$5,452,072
Turned pro: 2018
Residence: Villena, Spain
Siblings: Alvaro Alcaraz Garfia, Jaime Alcaraz Garfia, Sergio Alcaraz Garfia

Sponsors

Alcaraz already has a clothing and shoe sponsorship contract with Nike. In his amateur career, he was first sponsored by Lotto, and then in 2015 Nike swooped in.
Alcaraz signed his first professional contract with Nike in January 2020. On court, he is always seen wearing the Nike Air Zoom Vapor Pros on his feet.
